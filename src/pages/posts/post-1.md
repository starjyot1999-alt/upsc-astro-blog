# How to Read The Hindu for UPSC

Reading the newspaper is a daily ritual for clearing the Civil Services Exam.

## Key Focus Areas
- **Editorials:** Vital for Mains exam perspectives and essay writing.
- **National News:** Look for government schemes, polity, and governance issues.
- **Economy:** Track RBI policies and macroeconomic trends.
