# Introduction to the Indian Constitution

Welcome to your first study guide! The Constitution of India is the supreme law of India.

<ExamTip>
  Questions about the "Basic Structure" of the Constitution are frequently asked in GS Paper 2. Pay special attention to the difference between flexibility and rigidity when reading!
</ExamTip>

## Key Features
* **Lengthiest Written Constitution:** It is the most comprehensive constitution in the world.
* **Blend of Rigidity and Flexibility:** It can be amended, but core principles remain protected.
* **Parliamentary System:** Based on the British model of government.
> "The Constitution is not a mere lawyer's document, it is a vehicle of Life, and its spirit is always the spirit of Age." — B.R. Ambedkar
