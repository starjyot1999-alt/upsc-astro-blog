## Key Features of the Bill

### 1. Reservation
* **33% of directly elected seats** for women in:
  * Lok Sabha
  * All State Legislative Assemblies
  * Delhi Legislative Assembly
* **Not applicable to:**
  * Rajya Sabha
  * State Legislative Councils

### 2. New Articles Inserted
* **Art. 330A:** 33% of Lok Sabha seats (including 1/3 of SC/ST seats) for women.
* **Art. 332A:** Same scheme for Assemblies & Delhi Assembly.
* **Art. 334A:** Mode, timing, rotation, and duration of reservation.

### 3. Implementation
* **Effective only after:**
  * First census after commencement
  * Delimitation based on that census
* *Note: Gazetted into force on 16 April 2026, but not yet operational.*

### 4. Rotation and Duration
* **Rotation:** Women’s reserved seats will rotate after each delimitation as per Parliament law.
* **Duration:** Reservation lasts 15 years from commencement (from 16 April 2026), extendable by law.

### 5. SC/ST Seats & Purpose
* **SC/ST Quota:** 1/3 of SC/ST-reserved seats reserved for women of SC/ST. The overall SC/ST quota remains unchanged.
* **Purpose:** Boost women’s political representation and advance gender equality in legislatures.
