# The Indus Valley Civilization

The Indus Valley Civilization (IVC) is one of the oldest civilizations in the world. 

## Key Characteristics
* **Town Planning:** Grid system with streets cutting across at right angles.
* **Drainage System:** Advanced covered drains for hygiene.
* **Economy:** Trade-based, with evidence of seals and standardized weights.
